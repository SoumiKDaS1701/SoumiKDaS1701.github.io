About Me

I'm a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Zimmerman Group at Department of Chemistry, University of Michigan. My research interests primarily lie in development of quantum chemical methodologies and applying them in various areas of chemistry including small-molecule catalysis, kinetic modelling and reaction discovery. Prior to this, I obtained my B.S. degree from Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India.
